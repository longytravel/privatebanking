Your World Today

Sound familiar?

These are the kinds of problems your team tackles every day. Each one is a potential hackathon project.

mail

Client Queries & Meeting Prep

Today: Reading emails, checking trust deeds, reviewing tax positions, drafting bespoke responses, manually logging interactions
With AI: Auto-summarise cases ahead of reviews, draft responses in the right tone, suggest next steps or escalations
description

Trust Deed Interpretation

Today: Manually reading deeds to confirm powers, restrictions, consents, beneficiary classes — re-reading the same trust over and over
With AI: Ask in plain English: "Can capital be distributed to adult grandchildren?" or "Who are the income beneficiaries?"
monitoring

Staff Manager: Process Monitoring & Coaching

Today: Staff Manager has no visibility into bottlenecks or delays, missing early warning signals, no proactive nudges when cases stall
With AI: Flag at-risk cases, suggest next actions, surface trends to the Staff Manager, coach staff with just-in-time prompts

Each one of these could be a working prototype by the end of hackathon day.
