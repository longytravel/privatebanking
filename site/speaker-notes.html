Slide 9
Your 4-Week Journey
  • Three 1-hour virtual sessions, then hackathon day.
  • Session 1 (w/c 27 Apr): Basics of AI — what LLMs and agents are, where they fit in banking.
  • Session 2 (w/c 4 May): Planning with AI — prompting, thinking with it, using it as a tool.
  • Session 3 (w/c 11 May): Your business problems — identify PB pain points, form teams, scope projects.
  • Hackathon (19 May): Build, demo, judge.
Slide 10
The Day
  • 10am–4pm. Quick kickoff, then straight into building.
  • Facilitators on hand throughout.
  • Lunch break with a lightning talk.
  • Demos and judging from 3pm — 3 min pitch, 2 min Q&A per team.
  • Awards and close at 4. Format is proven — every team delivered.
Slide 11
Why This Matters for Private Banking
  • 73% of workers use AI weekly — but only 29% rate themselves as advanced.
  • That gap is the opportunity AND the risk.
  • 1/3 of UK banks already piloting autonomous AI agents.
  • The orgs winning at AI aren't buying it — they're building with it.
  • This gives your team: AI literacy, competitive advantage, more time with clients.
